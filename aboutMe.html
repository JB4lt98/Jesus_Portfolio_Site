About me!

Small introduction

Hey there! My name is Jesus Baltazar. At the time of writing this I go to CUNY Hunter College. As for the reason I am making this site, I want to major in Computer Science (BA), and want something to log my work and work on something for my resume at the same time! Here is what I look like!

Why code?

My first introduction to the world of coding was in the 6th grade of middle school. I went to JHS 220 (John J Pershing), and my first language that I learned was HTML! Since then, I knew I wanted to do something with programming, I just did not know what.

I didn't receive much exposure to coding after the 6th grade, until my second year of High school, (I went to Brooklyn College Academy). I learned Python, and its relation to game development. This pushed me into wanting to get into that realm of programming. I tried to download game engines and learn them, but rather opted in for the more basic approach, learning more languages.

When I reached college, I knew I had been exposed to the world of code and was comfortable enough to where I wanted to pursue it on my own time as well as in school. At the time of writing this, I took one class on Python, and one class on C++, and on my own time, started to learn Javascript, and also practicing HTML and CSS. I intend to keep going on this path and introduce new projects and ideas to this site.

I initially entered college with the thought that I would be a Game Developer/Game Designer. The more things I was exposed to, and the more research I did, the more I decided to branch out. I still am interested in making games at some point, but in order to start somewhere, I want to tackle the challenges of Web Development, and getting used to the world of Front and Back End development.

What I plan to do in the future is work somewhere in the area of Front-End development. Working with HTML/CSS, some JavaScript and other frameworks, but also working with the idea of designing websites also interests me. As I continue to learn more about CSS and perfect my "vision" with designing, this website will be periodically updated with new looks and functionalities.

For now, though, I am in college, on the hunt for internships, and working on my own projects!
